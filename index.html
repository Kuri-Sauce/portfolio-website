My Work

WatchGuide

Inspired by my own frustrations with having to hop from one streaming service app to the other in order to find the shows and movies I want to watch, I designed the universal app to allow all streaming content to be in one place. Using the iOS and Material Design guidelines, this design highlights the main actions, remains familiar to users by mirroring the other streaming app layouts for an easy transition, and features colors to create a movie watching ambiance.

Wireframing, prototyping

Tasty Vegan

User research suggested many people do not like finding recipes that require them to go shopping for required ingredients outside of their normal shopping routine and would rather find a recipe based on the ingredients they already have in their kitchen. Tasty Vegan gives the users their own virtual kitchen to include their available ingredients, kitchen tools, and customizable recipe book with a search engine that can provide recipes based on what is in their virtual kitchen.

User research, user flows, wireframing, prototyping, icon design
