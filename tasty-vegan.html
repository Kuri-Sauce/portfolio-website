My goal for this recipe app site is to help people with various cooking skills easily find tasty plant-based recipes they can cook based on their resources (ingredients, tools, local food stores), and in turn, they will hopefully have a more peaceful cooking experience.

User Research

Interview Hypothesis

Providing an ingredient search engine for new plant-based cooks will provide a smoother transition into plant-based cooking because they will be able to find recipes with ingredients they are already familiar with and easily accessible.

Questions

  • What do you enjoy about cooking? Do you enjoy trying new recipes?
  • When was the last time you challenged yourself in the kitchen?
  • Walk me through your approach to discovering plant-based recipes or any other new diet recipes.
  • What does planning a meal look like for you? (Anytime, whether it’s once a week, right before you cook, etc)
  • What are your experiences with finding the ingredients for plant-based recipes?
  • Do you have any favorite recipe apps, websites, and/or books? Tell me more about them.
  • What features do you think would be most useful to you in a recipe web app?

MVP Objective

To allow users of various cooking skills to easily search and compare recipes based on their local resources to allow for an easier transition into a plant-based diet.

User Stories & Feature Requirements

User Story

As a low-experienced cook, I want to try new recipes with ingredients familiar to me because I get bored eating the same thing every day.

Needed Requirements

The ability to compare recipes containing the same ingredients by providing a list of options. And have a large quantity of plant-based recipes.

User Story

As a busy therapist, I want to have quick access to saved recipes to make a fresh meal within 10 minutes, so that I can have plenty of time to eat and take a proper mental break.

Needed Requirements

Kitchen Inventory Page

  • Users can keep track of their ingredients in a virtual kitchen.
  • Can filter recipes by ingredients and tools in their virtual kitchen.
  • Ability to organize saved recipes for convenient access (based on mood, timing, etc).

User Story

As a person who does not plan meals ahead of time, I want to search for recipes based on the ingredients I have at home so that I do not spend so much time looking for a recipe that contains ingredients I do not have.

Needed Requirements

Search bar with the ability to search a collection of keywords when using the filters.

Hypothesis

Providing the option to search and compare by a collection of ingredients for users of all skill levels will result in users finding recipes that meet their expectations and needs.

Providing visual aids in recipes for the users will result in users feeling more confident in their transition into cooking plant-based meals.

Success Metrics

Quantitative

  • Ratings and comments
  • Number of times a recipe is shared and saved
  • Most-used search criteria
  • Number of different recipes shared and saved per user recipe collection sizes

Qualitative

  • Feedback submitted by users

MVP Iteration 2

In the event the MVP is successful, the following iterations will be the next to consider.

User Story

As a visual learner, I want to easily see what the finished recipe looks like and the steps included because I want to feel more engaged when learning something new.

Wanted Requirements

Clear visual aids (photos and videos) coupled with easy-to-follow recipe steps.

User Story

When I have time to browse recipes, I want to see where I can buy nearby ingredients listed in the recipe and add them to my grocery list so that I can easily plan.

Wanted Requirements

Allow users to click on ingredients and the sidebar pops up with useful information including where to buy it and add it to a grocery list.

User Story

As a beginner, I want to be able to filter out recipes that meet me at my skill level, because anything too challenging will turn me away from wanting to try.

Wanted Requirements

Provide users the option to fill out a quick survey to get an idea of their skill level so that their searches will provide recipes that match.
